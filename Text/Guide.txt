UP key is for jumping and DOWN key is for rapid fall. Use these two keys to
control the character's behavior and avoid game obstacles like grabs.
The player's initial physical power value is 100. With the progress
of the game, the physical power will slowly decrease. When the physical
strength value decreases to 1, the player needs to stop and rest,
and the game is over. Therefore, you need to obtain food items to maintain
physical strength. In the process of the game, you will encounter gold coin
reward props. After eating these gold coin reward props, you can obtain gold coins,
and use these gold coins to buy props in the mall to improve your role.
