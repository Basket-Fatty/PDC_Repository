As a runner, your dream is to run everywhere in the world.
Now, you have the opportunity to choose a role in the game to help you fulfill your dream.
In the game, you will encounter various obstacles,
All you have to do is try to cross these obstacles and go further to get higher points.
Of course, just like real characters, running for a long time will become tired.
You need to get food props in the game to supplement your physical strength.
In addition, in the game, we also set up gold coins to encourage those who love running and insist on running.
